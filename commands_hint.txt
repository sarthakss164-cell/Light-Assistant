for opening apps:
    light open ____
    special cases for open youtube and whatsapp:
        light open youtube
        light open whatsapp
for searching on web/chrome:
    light search about ____
for search on youtube:
    light search on youtube about ____
for sending messages on whatsapp:
    Note: whatsapp should be logged in on chrome
    light message {name} {message}
for playing video from youtube:
    Note: you have to copy that video link and that should be the latest link in clipboard
    light play from the link
for playing a song:
    light play ____
for pausing system while it is playing video or song:
    Note: Video or Song must be playing
    light pause/off
for increasing the volume of light:
    light increase volume
for decreasing the volume of light:
    light decrease volume
for stopping the music:
    Note: Music must be playing
    light stop music
for stopping the video:
    Note: Video must be playing
    light stop video
for stopping light:
    light stop/exit
